Welcome To Gas Booking And Delivery System

A gas cylinder is one of the most important utilities for us. Whether it is for our home or our for commercial use such as in a restaurant. But who likes to go outside and stand in the queues for hours just to book a gas cylinder. Feel the comfort of your home and book cylinder without any hassles following a few easy steps with Online Gas Booking Delivery service for all gas in side Kathmandu Valley.


Advantages of Online LPG Gas Booking  And Delivery

Online gas booking service  comes with many advantages which are as follows-

  • It is easy, convenient and gets processed within a minute
  • There are no hidden or additional charges
  • You can book a gas cylinder from anywhere and anytime in Kathmandu valley 
  • You can even see the booking detail of gas you have booked till now


    • How to Booked Gas?

    You can easily book a cylinder at GBDS by following these simple steps-

  • Go to the gas booking page then select available gas at the navigation bar
  • Then search the gas you desired  or select from the list
  • After selecting gas enter the correct location .
  • Your booking has been done and your gas is on the way.
